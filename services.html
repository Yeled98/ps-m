Cabinet Repainting and Refinishing

Painting cabinets requires a systematic approach to ensure a smooth and durable finish that can stand up to daily use. The process begins with removing the cabinet doors, drawers, and all hardware, including handles, hinges, and knobs. This step allows for a more thorough and precise paint job without any obstructions.
