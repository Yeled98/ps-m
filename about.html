From Our Family to Yours

Painting Solutions and More is a proud,
family-owned business rooted
in our local community.

Founded in 2008 by Gabriel Taveras, the company began with his years of property maintenance experience and the mentorship of a seasoned painter. Through a commitment to quality work and a reputation built solely through word-of-mouth referrals, the business steadily grew. By 2015, it had expanded enough for Gabriel's wife, Ingrid, to join and manage office administration, adding a personal, family-oriented touch.

In 2018, their son Nate Taveras brought his sales expertise from the paint manufacturing industry to the team. With Gabriel's in-the-field experience, Ingrid's leadership in the office, and Nate's in-depth product knowledge, Painting Solution And More grew to support five full-time crews. We take pride in delivering a customer-first experience, ensuring that each project—whether a luxury home repaint or a small commercial upgrade—is completed with care and professionalism.

At Painting Solution and More, we’re more than just painters; we're your neighbors. Licensed and insured, we are dedicated to creating lasting relationships by exceeding expectations on every project. From residential interiors to commercial exteriors, and everything in between, our goal is to transform your space and make you a lifelong customer. We're here to stay and serve our community for many years to come.
